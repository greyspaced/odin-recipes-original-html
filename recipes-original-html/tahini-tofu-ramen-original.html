Tahini Tofu Ramen

Description

Savory, spicy, saucy, and nutty noodle dish. Topped with crispy tofu.

Ingredients

  • boiled and drained ramen noodles
  • buldak sauce
  • tahini
  • sesame seeds
  • fried tofu
  • chopped green onions
  • left over ramen water (optional)

Steps

  1. Place noodles in serving bowl.
  2. Add buldak hot sauce to liking
  3. Add about a tablespoon of tahini. Can add more or less to your liking. Mix the tahni before hand with the leftover hot ramen water to make incorporating with the sauce easier.
  4. Mix noodles with the sauce.
  5. Top off the noodles with the tofu, sesame seeds, and green onions.
  6. Serve hot. Enjoy!
