Tea for Blue

Description

Hot tea, slightly sweet. Meant to warm up a cold Blue :)

Ingredients

  • Dried chamomile tea leaves
  • boiled hot water
  • 3/4 tsp sugar
  • clear glass cup

Steps

  1. Put leaves into tea strainer.
  2. Add hot water to the leaves.
  3. Leave to steep for 3-5 minutes.
  4. Pour steeped liquid in clear glass cup.
  5. Stir in sugar
  6. Serve hot and with head pats. Enjoy!
